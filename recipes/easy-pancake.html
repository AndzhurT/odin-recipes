Easy Pancake

Description

This easy pancake recipe doesn't require much thought early in the morning and the pancakes taste great!

Ingredients

  • Flour: (1 cup all-purpose flour) - This quick pancake recipe starts with a cup of all-purpose flour.
  • Sugar: (2 tablespoons white sugar) - You'll need two tablespoons of white sugar.
  • Baking Powder: (2 teaspoons baking powder) - Baking powder acts as a leavening agent, ensuring perfectly fluffy pancakes.
  • Salt: (1 teaspoon salt, or to taste) - Salt enhances the overall flavor of the pancakes, but it won't make the pancakes taste salty.
  • Milk and Oil: (1 cup milk and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil) - Milk and oil add moisture and flavor to the pancake batter.
  • Egg: (1 egg, beaten) - A beaten egg lends more moisture and helps bind the batter together.

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  4. Repeat with remaining batter
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
