Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie Recipe

Description

This yogurt smoothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries: (1 cup strawberries)
  • Banana: (1 banana)
  • Yogurt: (½ cup yogurt)
  • Pineapple and Orange Juice: (¼ cup pineapple juice)
  • White Sugar: (1 and ½ teaspoons white sugar)
  • Milk: (1 teaspoon milk)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
